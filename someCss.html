How Unusual

It is difficult to understand as to why this here, that which you preceive at this very moment, would be considered 'unassuming'. There is no real reason as to why this would be called as such; however, it is. The question that should be asked is no 'why', but rather 'when'. But why 'when' and not 'why'? Before your eyes once laid an unassuming mass of white and black. Therefore, it was usassuming.
